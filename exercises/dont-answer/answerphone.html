Don't Answer the Phone

You stare at the phone wildly with confusion, but you have to think quick. You decide to answer and hear muffled instructions. You pick up the phone. A nervous voice whispers, "Don't let it see you. Go to the basement and lock the door." Before you can ask any questions, the line goes dead. Another thump shakes the house. You must make a choice.

Go into the basement. Hang up and leave house.
